3.1 Review the benefits and drawbacks of IT tools and systems used in terms of productivity and efficiency – could you have been more efficient when making your website? How? Could you have done it in teams?

The benefits of using a dediacted software tool such as Visual Studios Code
for coding is that it allows you to productivly and efficiently troubleshoot
errors in the code with minimal eyestrain and sacrifice to time. It can support
rapid collaboration between multiple induviduals and expose the finished product
to a wider audience.

One of the most notable drawbacks of IT tools and systems is that they require
a degree of specialisation of knowledge to effectively use, restricting the
usability to those who are already familiar with the software or those who
are willing to invest resources to familiarise the team with the
given software/ IT tool.

The developement of this project could have been more efficient if a more
organised approach to structuring this project was undertaken before the
coding begun. Trello was used to aid this but due to there being a vague
marking criteria and an uncomfortable emphasis on creativity and personalisation
a more brute force method was employed to encourage greater productivity with the
only minor drawback being a sacrifice of
physical, emotional and mental wellbeing from the developer.



3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

It is widely debated as to the best method to administer caffiene to the brain
but a steady supply of coffee and energy drinks rationed throughout the day has
proven to be one of the more reliable methods to increase productivity albeit
to the occasional detriment to efficientcy.

Some softwares/IT tools can be employed effectively to improve an induviduals
productivity but aside from the ones that directly reduce the total workload
of the developer it can be dismissed as a self induced placebo effect - for
those who don't wish to commit to the grueling endeavour of actually
improving the function of the host's brain through the dedicated and
disciplined practice of exercise, diet, sleep and caffiene consumption.



3.3 Develop solutions to improve own productivity using IT in digital projects – what would you do differently next time? Team work? Use of Slack?

The major detriment to the productivity of this project was personal health and
out of work commitments. If these factors were more greatly considered prior to the
undertaking of the project no improvements to productivity would have likely been needed.

In regards to teamwork and slack: It may have been possible to imporve the total work load
undertaken by dividing up the tasks using a communication IT tool/software such as Slack or
zoom but without further dedication to organisation and project methodology the benefits may
not have provided notable improvements during the span of such a short project.
